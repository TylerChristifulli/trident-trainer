12 weeks = two 6-week mesocycles (RP-style). Each meso: 5 weeks of accumulating volume with intensity ramping from 3 RIR down to 0 RIR, then a deload. Lifting targets auto-progress from your logged sessions — hit the top of a rep range and the app bumps the load; your post-workout feedback adds or removes sets the following week.
